Damsel

Damsel is the new place where mobile singles are able to find each other, go on adventures, and build relationships.

It’s who you’re with and where you go.

Damsel is a travel app with love in mind.

Safety comes first

Not only are members anonymously rated for chivalry and quality, you and your partner are required to do a video call with each other before you may book one of our handcrafted adventures.
 
All adventures come with a local guide or are part of a tour group. Our support line is available 24/7 if you are ever in a pickle.

Choose the extraordinary

Another coffee date or witnessing the wildebeest migration in Africa. Explore new cultures, try new foods, and see something you can't by spending more money locally.
 
Your time is finite and valuable, so choose what makes you feel alive.

More than beauty

Most dating apps are all about looks. Here, we focus on the person: Education, values, personality.
 
We even made it simple by assigning everyone a princess or prince personality type. Once you know your type, match them, and create your own fairy-tale journey.

Why did we make this?

  • So you can explore the world and other cultures
  • So you aren't limited to only dating people in your city
  • So you can love dating and go on adventures with someone you like
  • So you'll go on great dates by filtering out bad dates through video chat

How does it work?

Once you receive your invite, you’ll be able to match with mobile singles from anywhere, not just in your city. After you match, you’ll need to match again on an adventure.
 
If you do, it must be fate! Book one of our designer adventures after you video chat each other. Unmatch anytime for any reason.

What's the fine print?

All adventures are with other people for your safety and may be domestic or international. Costs range from $300-$3,500. Each person must pay for his or herself. A $50 fully refundable deposit on the trip is required before videochat. Call our agents to finalize your booking.
 
Be careful! Trips may change your life.

We hope this app opens you up to the most amazing people. The world is more international than ever. Your phone isn't the product of just one country. Your food came from thousands of miles away. Why should the most important thing in your life be any different?
 
We believe who you want is out there, maybe just beyond your castle walls. The world is a miraculous place. Match, match, and away!
